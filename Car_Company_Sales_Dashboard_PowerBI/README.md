# Car Company Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive sales dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI to analyze the sales performance of a car company. The dashboard helps visualize key business metrics and identify trends in product sales and revenue.

## Objective

To analyze car sales data and generate insights that support better business decisions in terms of product performance, sales trends, and market demand.

## Dataset

The dataset contains information related to:

* Car models
* Sales values
* Revenue distribution
* Product performance

## Key Dashboard Insights

* Overall sales performance
* Revenue distribution across car models
* Identification of top-selling vehicles
* Comparative sales analysis
* Trend analysis of car sales
