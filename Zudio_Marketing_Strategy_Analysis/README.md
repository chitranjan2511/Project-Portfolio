# Zudio Marketing Strategy Analysis (4P Framework)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the marketing strategy of Zudio, a fast-growing fashion retail brand in India, using the 4Ps marketing framework: Product, Price, Place, and Promotion.

## Objective

To understand how Zudio positions itself in the competitive retail fashion industry and how its marketing mix helps it attract mass-market consumers.

## Product Strategy

Zudio offers a wide product mix including:

* Men's wear
* Women's wear
* Kids' wear
* Footwear
* Accessories

The brand maintains consistency by focusing on trendy and affordable fashion products targeted toward price-conscious consumers.

## Pricing Strategy

Zudio follows a **penetration pricing strategy**, offering products at lower prices than competitors.

Example price comparison:

* Zudio T-Shirts: ₹99–₹499
* Max: ₹199–₹799
* Pantaloons: ₹399–₹999

This strategy helps Zudio capture a large market share.

## Distribution Strategy

Zudio relies heavily on physical retail stores located in:

* Shopping malls
* High streets
* Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities

The company uses a **Franchise-Owned Company-Operated (FOCO)** model to expand rapidly.

## Promotion Strategy

Zudio promotes its brand through:

* Social media marketing
* Influencer collaborations
* Targeted digital advertisements
* Seasonal promotions and sales

## Key Insights

Zudio’s growth is driven by aggressive pricing, efficient supply chains, and rapid retail expansion across India.
